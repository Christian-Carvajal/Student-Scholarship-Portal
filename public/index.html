UPHSD Molino Scholarship Desk

Basic Qualification Criteria

To be eligible for the scholarship programs we offer, applicants must meet the general requirements set by partner organizations such as the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), Department of Science and Technology (DOST), Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), SM Foundation, Ayala Foundation, and Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO).

In general, applicants must be Filipino citizens who have completed or are currently enrolled in senior high school or college. Most programs require strong academic performance, typically ranging from a general weighted average of 80% to 93%, depending on the scholarship. Financial need is also a major consideration, with income limits applied to ensure assistance is given to deserving students.

Some scholarships are designed for specific groups, such as STEM students, coconut farmer families, female students, or individuals pursuing technical and vocational training. Applicants must also comply with course-specific and program-specific requirements where applicable.

IMPORTANT REMINDER

Important Reminder for Students

Before applying, students are encouraged to carefully review the requirements, benefits, and conditions of each scholarship program. Since each scholarship differs in eligibility, coverage, duration, and renewal policies, it is important to confirm that you meet all qualifications before submitting your application.

  • Prepare all required documents in advance, including academic records, proof of income, and valid identification.
  • Meet all application deadlines to avoid delays or disqualification due to late submission.
  • Maintain good academic standing and follow program rules, including grade requirements, participation duties, or return service obligations when required.

Scholarship We Offer

SM College Scholarship

Amount per year: ~₱80K - ₱120K

Offered by SM Foundation, this scholarship supports incoming college students with strong academic performance and financial need. It covers full tuition, provides monthly allowance, leadership training, and possible employment opportunities within SM after graduation.

Megaworld Foundation Scholarship

Amount per year: ~₱70K - ₱120K

The Megaworld Foundation offers scholarships to students in partner schools, especially those taking business, IT, and engineering courses. It provides full or partial tuition, allowance, and career opportunities within Megaworld companies.

Ayala U-Go Scholarship

Amount per year: ~₱60K - ₱120K

Supported by Ayala Foundation, this program focuses on female students with strong academic potential and financial need. It provides tuition support, living allowance, and mentorship programs.

Aboitiz Scholarship Program

Amount per year: ~₱60K - ₱100K

Offered by Aboitiz Foundation, this scholarship supports students in priority fields such as engineering, business, and IT. It includes tuition assistance, leadership training, and opportunities to work within Aboitiz companies after graduation.

Required Documents

Prepare TWO (2) Sets:

  • SET 1 Authenticated / Certified true copies and photocopies - for submission.
  • SET 2 Original copies - for office verification.
For Renewing Applicants

Required documents:

  1. Enrollment Proof - Certificate of Enrollment or Registration Form (PDF).
  2. Academic Proof - latest Form 138 or TOR (PDF).
  3. Proof of Identity - valid government-issued ID or PSA Birth Certificate (PDF/JPG/JPEG/PNG).
For New Applicants

Required documents:

  1. Enrollment Proof - Certificate of Enrollment or Registration Form (PDF).
  2. Academic Proof - latest Form 138 or TOR (PDF).
  3. Proof of Identity - valid government-issued ID or PSA Birth Certificate (PDF/JPG/JPEG/PNG).
  4. Proof of Income - ITR, Tax Exemption, or Certificate of Indigency (PDF/JPG/JPEG/PNG).
  5. Character Reference - Certificate of Good Moral (PDF).
  6. Recent Photo - 2x2 ID picture (JPG/JPEG/PNG).
Additional Supporting Documents (If Applicable)
  1. Proof of Income for all earning household members.
  2. Updated Character Reference from adviser/dean.
  3. Recent Photo with white background and name label.
  4. Enrollment load summary for unit verification.
  5. Academic standing summary from the previous term.
  6. Identity revalidation if submitted ID is expired.
List of acceptable IDs: School ID Passport National ID Driver's License Voter's ID PhilHealth ID Postal ID Senior / PWD ID

Maintaining Your Scholarship Status

Core Requirements

  • Complete and submit all required application or renewal forms within the official schedule of the scholarship provider.
  • Maintain a passing academic standing and avoid failing, incomplete, or dropped subjects.
  • Follow all rules, guidelines, and verification processes set by the scholarship provider.

Offense and Penalty

  • 1st Probation / Warning
  • 2nd Suspension from the scholarship for the semester
  • 3rd Termination from the scholarship
