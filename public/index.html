The Student Scholarship Program at UPHSD Molino is designed to honor the hard work and academic dedication of our students. Available Scholarship Programs

Entrance Scholarship

Exclusive to incoming freshmen who graduated as Top 1 to 10 of their Senior High School batch.

Academic Scholarship

Awarded to currently enrolled students who maintain a superior GWA in the preceding semester.

Tamayo Grants

Special financial assistance for students who demonstrate both academic potential and financial need.

Performing Arts & Athletics

Grants for students who represent the university in cultural and sporting excellence.

Automated Eligibility Criteria

To ensure a factual and merit-based ranking, our system utilizes the following GWA tiers for Academic Scholarships:

  • Platinum Scholar (100% Tuition Discount): GWA of 1.00 – 1.20
  • Gold Scholar (50% Tuition Discount): GWA of 1.21 – 1.50
  • Silver Scholar (25% Tuition Discount): GWA of 1.51 – 1.75

Note: Applicants must have no grade lower than 85% in any subject and must carry a regular load as per the curriculum checklist.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the automated ranking work? +
Rankings are calculated dynamically based on your cumulative GPA compared against the minimum threshold set by the system administrator.
Can I apply for multiple scholarships? +
Yes, as long as you meet the eligibility requirements for each specific fund. Ensure your essays are tailored appropriately.
Who is eligible for the UPHSD Gold Tier grant? +
You must have completed at least 2 full semesters as a regular student with no record of disciplinary action.
What file types are accepted for requirements? +
Transcripts and letters should be uploaded as PDF. Student ID may be uploaded as JPG/PNG or PDF.
